Frequently Asked Questions

How often do tours run?

Underground tours run Monday, Wednesday, and Friday evenings. Sherlock Holmes tours run Tuesday and Thursday evenings, and Saturday afternoons. Halloween tours run ever evening throughout October, with extra tours running on the day of Halloween.

Do you run private tours for groups/ parties etc.?

Yes - we can accomodate large groups, special events and parties. Please get in touch with us and let us know what you need.

What about the weather?

Tours run come wind, rain or snow, so please dress appropriately for the conditions! Your tour guide will be waiting for you whatever the weather.
