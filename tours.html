Tube Station Tours

Strange figures. Odd noises. Unexplained happenings. Ghostly goings-on have been reported on the Underground since its opening in 1863. Experience them for yourself with a tour covering the most supernatural subterranean stations in London. Locations include Covent Garden, Bank, Elephant & Castle, and Bethnal Green.

Try a haunted Tube tour, and you'll have one more reason to dread the morning commute.

Sherlock Holmes Tours

Sleuth your way around London, visiting locations made famous in Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's books, and TV and film adaptations. The tour begins in Piccadilly where Holmes and Watson first meet in the books, then takes in a variety of literary locations including grand hotels and gentleman's clubs, before finishing at Somerset House.

Dressing up is not necessary, but is highly encouraged!

Jack the Ripper Tours

Follow in the footsteps of the legendary Ripper himself on this bloodcurdling tour of the East End. Discover key locations from the myth, including the murder sites of the Canonical Five victims, and learn the identities of the prime suspects in the case. Expect sinister twists and turns as you experience the terror of Victorian Whitechapel.

This tour is not recommended for young children due to its dark and violent subject matter.
